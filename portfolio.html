All About Amber:

Amber was born on August 24, 2002 in Los Angeles, CA. She has an 11 year old sister named Ashley and a dog named Teddy. She is half Salvadorian and half white. Amber currently lives in Northridge, CA and is a rising senior at Granada Hills Charter High School. She is in Cohort 7 of the International Baccalaureate Program and loves the program. In the future, she hopes to become an aerospace engineer, adopt 2 cats, a dog, and live on the beach.

Learn More About her:

Amber likes to run with her Cross Country and Track and Field Team. In Cross Country, she and her teammates won 1st in LA City Finals for Varisty Girls and competed at the State competition.

She also plays violin, both as a soloist and in an orchestra. Amber started playing violin when she was 9, inspired by her grandmother, a professional violinist. As a soloist, she has competed in a few competitions. Colburn String Orchestra, and, currently, the CSUN Youth Philharmonic, conducted under Dr. Roscigno. She is also currently working with a small ensemble at CSUN to raise money for the food pantry.

Amber volunteers avidly at her church, from lectoring to altar serving to singing as a choir member. Amber is also very much so a kid at heart and loves to volunteer with them. Through various volunteering for her project Stembassdors, she hangs out teaches STEM lessons to those without access.
